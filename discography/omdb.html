OVER MY DEAD BODY


Everybody knows I'm broken down to the bone.

The nine track debut studio album. A journey of realization and self discovery, arrogance and poetry, ego and despair. An evolution in sound with familiarity becoming overshadowed by rock elements and a fresh voice. A revelation.
