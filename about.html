get to know alaska sargent

Alaska Sargent is a Maine musician.

With over 600 thousand global streams across platforms, Sargent is solidifying her identity as a musical powerhouse.

As Alaska Sargent, she has dedicated her songwriting to introspection. "era1/circles/archive" is an archival collection of 12 tracks stemming from her career's infancy, featuring collaborations with the likes of saoirse dream.

After a period of self-expulsion and rehabilitation, Sargent released her debut studio album, OVER MY DEAD BODY, a deeply personal intimate record summarizing a cycle of guilt, betrayal and bittersweet. OVER MY DEAD BODY has received 50 thousand global streams across platforms, Sargent hosted a successful crowdfund campaign for vinyl records of the release, and compact discs are currently being sold in Bull Moose locations in Maine.

In five years, Sargent has caught the eyes of many indie figures, with festival appearances alongside bands like Oolong, Michael Cera Palin and Weatherday, mentions on multiple news publications and exclusive music premieres on stations such as FBi Radio, Richmond Independent Radio and WJTB Radio.
